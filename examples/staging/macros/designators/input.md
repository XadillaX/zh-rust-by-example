The arguments of a macro are prefixed by a dollar sign `$` and type annotated
with a *designator*:

{designators.play}

This is a list of all the designators:

* `block`
* `expr` is used for expressions
* `ident` is used for variable/function names
* `item`
* `pat` (*pattern*)
* `path`
* `stmt` (*statement*)
* `tt` (*token tree*)
* `ty` (*type*)
